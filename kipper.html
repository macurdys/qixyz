The Problem

X Has No Science Filter

Community Notes is crowd-sourced opinion. Scientific claims on X are judged by popularity, not evidence. Misinformation spreads at the speed of engagement while corrections arrive too late and get buried. There's no mechanism to distinguish a PhD researcher from a bot farm.

The Kipper Solution

Proof-of-Knowledge Validation

Kipper overlays a scientific consensus layer on X feeds. Trusted validators — verified by Quai Network's Proof-of-Entropy-Minima — annotate, challenge, and verify scientific claims in real-time. The Verified Science badge is earned, not bought. Energy-backed credibility.
