Bay 01 — Mission

Energy is money.
Build on it.

Qi is a currency minted proportionally to the energy miners spend securing the Quai Network. It is not pegged to a fiat price. It is pegged to physics — to the thermodynamic cost of computation.

This station exists to build the economy that runs on Qi. Startups. Apps. Games. Videos. Ideas. Everything here is oriented toward one question: what does the world look like when money is energy?
