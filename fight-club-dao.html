The Rules

Rules of Fight Club DAO

Eight rules. No exceptions. Memorize them.

01 You do talk about Fight Club DAO. Spread the word. That's the whole point.
02 You DO talk about Fight Club DAO. Seriously — onboard everyone.
03 Someone says "I don't get crypto" — the fight is over. You failed. Try again next week.
04 Only one mission per week. Focus. Finish it. Submit proof.
05 One fight at a time. Don't rush ahead. Each op builds on the last.
06 No proof, no XP. Screenshots, tx hashes, photos. Verifiable or it didn't happen.
07 Fights go on as long as they have to. The calendar runs 12 weeks. Keep showing up.
08 If this is your first night at Fight Club DAO — you have to fight. Week 01 is mandatory.
Protocol

How the Fight Works

One mission drops per week. Execute it in the real world. Prove it. Rank up. Repeat until you're a legend.

01

Mission Drops

Every Monday at 00:00 UTC a new mission briefing goes live. Clear objective. Clear rules. Seven days to execute.

02

Fight & Prove

Complete the mission in the real world or on-chain. Capture evidence — screenshot, tx hash, photo, conversation log. No proof, no credit.

03

Rank Up

Community validates proofs. Completed fights earn XP toward your rank. Stack wins, unlock governance, climb the leaderboard.
