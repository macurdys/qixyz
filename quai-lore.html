THE OPERATOR
Thermodynamic Engineer / Network Architect
Visual Identity
Red Quai cap with "Q" emblem. Long hair, full beard. Lab coat over dark base layers. Aviator sunglasses with red-tinted lenses. Server room / data center environments.
Archetype
Part scientist, part builder, part cypherpunk. Harnesses raw computational energy to forge a new monetary system rooted in thermodynamic truth.
Motivation
Believes money must be backed by physical energy — not faith, not fiat, not consensus alone. Every hash is a vote cast in joules.

Near-future cyberpunk city. Q-branded skyscrapers. Lightning storms and energy beams. The grid is alive.
