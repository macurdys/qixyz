Framework

The Economic Triad

Every transaction in an AI-mediated economy involves three parties: the Human who desires, the Agent who acts, and the Energy that makes action possible. The Moral Compass helps navigate this relationship.

H
HUMAN

The principal. Sets intention, provides oversight, holds ultimate accountability. Bears the moral weight of decisions.

A
AGENT

The executor. Translates intention into action, optimizes within constraints, operates under delegated authority.

E
ENERGY

The substrate. The thermodynamic cost of computation. In Qi, this is money itself — work made legible.

Dynamics

Value Flow

The compass points not to cardinal directions but to economic relationships. Understanding how value flows between Human, Agent, and Energy is the foundation of ethical AI economics.

Primary Economic Loop
HUMAN
→
AGENT
→
ENERGY

The Human delegates to the Agent. The Agent consumes Energy to act. The result flows back as value — completing the circuit. Qi makes this loop transparent: energy expenditure is visible, accountable, priced in physics.
