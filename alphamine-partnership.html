Mining Meets Development

AlphaMine is a mining operation with deep technical expertise in logistics and strategy across a range of systems; solar, battery, proof-of-work, zk-proving, and mining pools. American Energy Money is a newly established infrastructure devevelopment corp focused on the expansion strategy and economics of energy-based monetary systems. Together, their partnership will focus on physical expansiono of solar production and mining operations. Development will occur on a 3-acre site in Southampton, NJ.

The timing makes sense. Since the release of the Subsidized, Open-Market Allocation Protocol (S.O.A.P), proof-of-work economics has changed significantly. AlphaMine has already optimized the operational side. American Energy Money is now expanding solar energy infrastructure on top of that operational foundation, which pushes those electricity costs even lower and creates a long-term hedge against rate volatility.

Why Now

The market conditions for this kind of alignment are unusually favorable right now. The investment thesis is simple: lower energy costs, expand mining hardware + algorithms, and expand future capacity through solar. AlphaMine has the operational track record and the existing partnerships to scale immediately. American Energy Money has the capital strategy to fund that expansion.

As the partnership expands, the plan is to find the most profitable strategies and power energy-based monetary systems by harvesting the sun's energy. It's time to build.
