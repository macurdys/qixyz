Missions Alignment

There's a moment in every industrial revolution where theory and practice collide — where the mathematics on the whiteboard meet steel in the ground and sunlight on silicon. That moment is here. American Energy Money and AlphaMine are joining forces to build the physical infrastructure for energy-based money.

The Mission

Mission — American Energy Money

AmericanEnergyMoney.com The American status as "world numeraire" doesn't necessarily have to fade into oblivion. But changes need to be made, quickly and responsibly, and adopting real Energy Based Money is a solution. It supports proof-of-work networks like Bitcoin, Quai Network (founded in America), and others.

Mission — AlphaMine

AlphaMine's mission is simple and profoundly ambitious: turn sunlight into Qi. Solar energy captured at the panel, converted to hash, and transmuted into proof-of-work — the cleanest, most direct path from photon to money that exists. No intermediaries. Just the sun, silicon, and SHA-256.

The Overlap

Our expertise overlaps precisely where it matters most — merging the theoretical with the commercial in the physical. American Energy Money proudly gains a commercial mining partner and will provide investment into the expansion of solar mining operations built by AlphaMine. Our core thesis is that energy-backed, proof-of-work money is the natural successor to fiat currency, grounded in thermodynamic law rather than political convenience.
